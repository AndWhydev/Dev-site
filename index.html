Inside our delivery pipeline

Multi-agent build pipeline. Senior engineers in review.

Overseer is our internal orchestration layer, it dispatches parallel Claude workers to deliver vertical slices, runs every change through our quality gate matrix (Hallmark design, Playwright visual, type-check, security scan), and holds at the deploy gate until a senior engineer signs off. It compresses delivery time without compressing standards.

How We Work

From Idea to Production

A delivery framework built for complex organisations, predictable, transparent and designed so nothing about how we work is a surprise.

01
1-2 weeks

Discovery & Strategy

We invest heavily in understanding your business before writing a line of code. Stakeholder workshops, technical audits, competitive analysis and requirements documentation, the foundation for everything that follows.

02
2-3 weeks

Architecture & Design

System architecture documents, database schemas, API contracts and high-fidelity UI prototypes. Every decision is documented and agreed before development begins, eliminating scope creep and costly rework.

03
4-16 weeks

Development & Build

Agile two-week sprints with daily standups, continuous integration and transparent progress tracking via your dedicated project portal. You see real progress every single day.

04
1-2 weeks

Quality Assurance

Automated and manual testing, performance benchmarking, security scanning and user acceptance testing. We don't ship code that isn't production-ready, no exceptions.

05
3-5 days

Launch & Deploy

Zero-downtime deployments, infrastructure provisioning, monitoring setup and go-live support. We manage the technical complexity so your team can focus on the business launch.

06
Ongoing

Support & Scale

SLA-backed support, proactive monitoring, regular performance reviews and a dedicated team ready to evolve your product as your business grows. We're with you for the long term.

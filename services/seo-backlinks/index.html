Overview

What does SEO & Backlinks involve?

SEO and backlinks is the practice of improving a website's technical foundations, content relevance and external authority so it earns sustainable visibility in search engines and AI-driven answer engines.

Search engine optimisation has a credibility problem, and it is largely self-inflicted. The industry is full of operators promising guaranteed first-page rankings, selling networks of low-quality links, and chasing tactics that work briefly before a search engine update wipes them out, often taking the client's visibility down with them. We take the opposite view. Durable search performance comes from the same things that make a website genuinely good: it loads fast, it is technically sound and crawlable, it answers real questions better than the alternatives, and other reputable sites reference it because it deserves to be referenced. We will not promise you rankings, because no honest practitioner can control a search engine's algorithm, but we can methodically remove the technical barriers holding you back, build content that earns its place, and acquire authority the way it is meant to be acquired.

Our work spans three connected areas. Technical SEO addresses the foundations: Core Web Vitals and page performance, crawlability and indexation, site architecture, canonicalisation, mobile usability, and Schema.org structured data that helps search engines and AI systems understand your content. We diagnose these with Google Search Console, Screaming Frog and Lighthouse, then fix them at the code level rather than papering over them with plugins. On-page and content SEO aligns your pages with genuine search intent and builds topical depth that establishes you as a credible source, increasingly important as search shifts toward AI-generated answers, where generative engine optimisation (GEO) means structuring content so large language models can cite it accurately. Finally, authority building through ethical link acquisition: digital PR, genuinely useful resources, relevant industry placements and strong local SEO signals including Google Business Profile and consistent citations. We explicitly do not engage in private blog networks, paid link schemes or any tactic that violates search engine guidelines, because the short-term gain is never worth the long-term penalty risk. We measure everything against GA4 and Search Console so you can see what is actually moving, and tie it back to business outcomes rather than vanity metrics.

We earn authority ethically through digital PR, genuinely useful resources, relevant placements and, where applicable, local citation and Google Business Profile work. Every link is one we would be comfortable explaining to a search engine reviewer.

We track performance in Search Console and GA4, report on organic traffic and conversions rather than vanity metrics, and iterate based on what the data shows. SEO is a compounding, ongoing discipline, and we are transparent about timelines and what is realistically achievable.

Key Terms, Defined

SEO & Backlinks: a quick glossary

Core Web Vitals
Google's set of user-experience metrics measuring loading performance, interactivity and visual stability of a page. They are a confirmed ranking signal and a proxy for real user experience.
Structured Data (Schema.org)
Standardised markup added to a page that helps search engines and AI systems understand its content and entities, enabling rich results and accurate citation in answer engines.
Crawlability
How easily search engine bots can discover, access and read the pages of a website. Crawl traps, blocked resources and poor internal linking all reduce crawlability and visibility.
GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)
The practice of structuring content and entities so AI-driven answer engines and large language models can understand, trust and accurately cite a business in generated responses.
Backlink
A link from another website to yours. Search engines treat reputable, relevant backlinks as votes of confidence that contribute to a site's authority, when earned ethically.
Local SEO
Optimisation focused on visibility in location-based searches and the map pack, driven by Google Business Profile, citation consistency and locally relevant content and signals.
